FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Growthlink actually build for me?+
We set up your complete client-acquisition system: landing pages/funnels, GoHighLevel CRM automations, ad campaigns, email/SMS follow-up sequences, and a structured sales process with setter-closer scripts.
What are the payment options?+
We offer flexible plans. For our B2C program, options start with a partial upfront payment with the remainder on a results-based or installment schedule. For B2B, we offer structured installment plans up to 5 months. Financing through Klarna, Sezzle (Canada), and PayPal Later (US) is also available.
Are there additional costs beyond the program fee?+
Yes — you'll need an ad budget (recommended minimum ~$25/day), a GoHighLevel subscription ($97/mo), and a domain purchase. We'll walk through all costs transparently on your strategy call.
How quickly can I expect to see results?+
Timelines vary by niche and ad spend, but most clients have their systems live within the first week of onboarding. Lead flow typically begins as soon as ads are launched and optimized.
What if I don't have a business idea yet?+
That's completely fine. During your strategy call, we'll help you identify a viable business model based on your skills, interests, and market demand before building anything.
