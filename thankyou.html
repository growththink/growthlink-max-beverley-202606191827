What happens on the strategy call?+
We'll review your current business situation, identify your biggest growth bottlenecks, and present a custom roadmap with the exact systems and steps needed to scale.
Can I reschedule if something comes up?+
Absolutely. Check your confirmation email for a reschedule link, or reach out to us directly and we'll find a new time that works.
Is there anything I need to prepare?+
Just come with a general idea of your goals, current revenue (if applicable), and any challenges you're facing. We'll handle the rest.

Not at all. The call is genuinely about understanding your situation and seeing if we can help. If it's a fit, we'll walk through options. If not, you'll still leave with a clear action plan.
